# ROGE
Roge is my one of the best pcb design's that I have made in the past two days this was done on EasyEDA its a simple stable oscillator or multi vibrator that has 3 LEDs , 2  330 ohm resistors that control my LEDs brightness and 2  6.2k ohm resistors that control the time delay of blinking of my LEDs and the I am gonna use a small cell coin for this so it is soo light weight and comfortable to carry around and this wouldn't feel bulky 

I used to think my skill in efficiently controlling four poin curves in MS Paint was a useless skill but when I was tracing the Outline it was sooooo satisfying and not hectic I wanna do this more and more and also next i would wanna try the multicolor pcb's and also make them more so i can give it to some of my close frd's . This is actually for build-guild-bengaluru but it was sooo funn and interesting that i would make more PCB accessories from now hehe :)

and the main part this was all abt my pcb but if you wanna make this on your own or even adjust the time delay for LED's blinking use this formula 
T = 2 * 0.68 * R * C
T is Desired Time Delay for LED's BLink
R is resistance 
C is capacitance
