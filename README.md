# ROGE
Roge is my one of the best pcb design's that I have made in the past two days this was done on EasyEDA its a simple stable oscillator or multi vibrator that has 3 LEDs , 2  330 ohm resistors that control my LEDs brightness and 2  6.2k ohm resistors that control the time delay of blinking of my LEDs and the I am gonna use a small cell coin for this so it is soo light weight and comfortable to carry around and this wouldn't feel bulky
